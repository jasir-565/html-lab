ABOUT MESCE:

MES College of Engineering (MESCE), Kuttippuram, established in 1994, is a self-financing, minority status engineering college in Kerala, affiliated with the University of Calicut and approved by AICTE. Located along the Bharathapuzha River, it offers various undergraduate, postgraduate, and PhD programs in fields like Engineering, Management, and Computer Applications. The college emphasizes technical education and social responsibility, with a focus on empowering students, particularly from minority and educationally backward communities
