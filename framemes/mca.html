DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER APPLICATION

The Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) Department at MES College of Engineering, Kuttipuram, offers B.Tech programs in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security), along with a Master of Computer Applications (MCA) postgraduate program. The department provides well-equipped labs, internet facilities, a library, and a dedicated faculty to support students' learning and research. It is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and is known for preparing graduates with strong computer science and cyber security skill s
